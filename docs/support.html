Health Signal Monitor

Support information for the Health Signal Monitor iPhone app.

Privacy Policy

Contact

For support questions, bug reports, or App Store issues, contact: healthsignalmonitor@gmail.com

Support is handled by email. Include enough detail for the issue to be reproduced without needing follow-up just to understand the problem.

What To Include In A Support Request

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • The app version and build number.
  • A short description of what happened.
  • Screenshots if the issue is visual or data-related.
  • Whether the issue involves Apple Health permissions or Apple Watch data.

What Support Can Help With

  • Questions about app behavior, Health access prompts, and chart values.
  • Bug reports, crashes, and incorrect metric displays.
  • How to delete local app data or reset onboarding-related state.
  • App Store download or update issues specific to this app.

Troubleshooting Basics

  • Make sure Apple Health access is enabled for the data types you want the app to read.
  • After changing Health permissions, reopen the app and refresh the data.
  • If values look outdated, confirm that Apple Health shows recent data for the same metric.
  • If needed, use the app’s local reset controls to clear local state and start fresh.

Important Product Boundary

Health Signal Monitor is designed to help users notice changes in personal health patterns and suggest follow-up steps when more data may be helpful. It is not a diagnostic tool, not emergency monitoring, and not a replacement for professional medical care.

If you believe you may be having a medical emergency, do not wait for app support. Contact local emergency services or a qualified clinician directly.
